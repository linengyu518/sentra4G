How it works

From a quiet shot to your inbox in seconds — no app required to receive.

The camera never holds your email credentials. It speaks only to the SENTRA relay, which delivers the photo to whatever address you set in the console.

STEP 01

Capture

You trigger a capture from your end. The 5MP sensor takes a sharp photo while the mic captures clear audio.

→
STEP 02

Relay

The image travels over 4G to the SENTRA relay with the unit's serial — encrypted, no SMTP on the device.

→
STEP 03

Deliver

The relay looks up your recipients and emails the photo. Change addresses any time — the device stays untouched.
